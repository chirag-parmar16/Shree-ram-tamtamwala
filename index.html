About Shri Ram Tamtamwala

The journey of Shri Ram Tamtamwala began in 1947 with late Shri Ram Durga Prasad Gupta. What started as a humble "Khumcha" (basket) seller in the narrow lanes of Wadi, Vadodara, has today become a legendary name in traditional Indian snacks.

For over seven decades, we have preserved the authentic taste of tradition. Our secret lies in the handpicked spices and time-honored recipes that have been passed down through generations, ensuring every bite of our famous "Tamtam" is an explosion of flavor.
