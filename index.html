About Shriram Tamtamwala

At Shriram Tamtamwala, our mission is to preserve the rich heritage of our traditional snacks. Established in 1947, we have been committed to delivering the highest quality products to our customers. Our story began with a passion for creating delicious snacks that bring joy and satisfaction to every bite.

We take pride in using only the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our dedication to quality and authenticity has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Join us on a journey through the flavors of tradition, and discover why Shriram Tamtamwala has been a household name for over seven decades.

Our key milestones:

  • 1947: The journey begins
  • 1975: Expansion to new markets
  • 2000: Introduction of new product lines
  • 2020: Reaching customers worldwide
