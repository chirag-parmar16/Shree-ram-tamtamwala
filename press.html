Legacy Noticed

A City Legend

From national dailies like The Times of India to local culinary journals, the story of Shree Ram Tamtamwala has been celebrated for over seven decades. We are honored to be recognized as a cornerstone of Vadodara's food culture.

Our legacy isn't just in the snacks we sell, but in the community we've built. These media features tell the story of a family's dedication to quality that has captured the hearts of food critics and locals alike.
